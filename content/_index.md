# BeLive Technology Secure SDLC

## Overview

This document outlines the Secure Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) at BeLive Technology. Our process is designed to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our systems and data, and to align with the requirements of ISO 27001.

## About BeLive Technology

BeLive Technology builds enterprise-grade live-streaming, short-video, shoppable-video, and interactive-video SaaS solutions. Key areas include livestreaming, short-video, SDKs for iOS/Android/Web, AI-based analytics, interactive commerce, and enterprise integrations.

## Development Methodology

We follow a hybrid approach that combines Agile and Waterfall methodologies to ensure both flexibility and control throughout the development process.

## Our Tools

We use a modern toolchain to support our SDLC:

*   **Version Control:** GitHub
*   **CI/CD:** GitHub Actions, Jenkins
*   **Project Management:** Jira
*   **Security Scanning:** Dependabot, OSV (local)
*   **Secrets Management:** AWS Secrets Manager

## ISO 27001 Focus

This SDLC process is designed to comply with ISO 27001, with a primary focus on the controls outlined in **Annex A.8 - Asset Management**.

## Audience

The primary audience for this documentation is ISO auditors.

## Scope

The scope of this framework is to secure the entire value stream of software development at BeLive Technology.
